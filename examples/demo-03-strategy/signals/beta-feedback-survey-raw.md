# Beta cohort raw feedback — collected via survey, April 2026

(8 of 20 beta accounts responded. Selected quotes.)

> "Pricing calculator on the landing page is confusing. Couldn't tell
> what we'd pay at 5x our current volume." — Account #3

> "Migration docs incomplete. We tried self-serve migration and hit a
> snag on users with mid-month plan changes." — Account #7

> "Loved the UX. Saw real ROI at 3x usage." — Account #11

> "We're not sure if we can stay on v1.5 or if you'll sunset it. Need
> clarity before committing to v2." — Account #14

> "Who's my point of contact? Got 3 emails from 3 different people
> this quarter." — Account #19

## Common themes

- Pricing clarity (not the price, but the UNDERSTANDING of the price)
- Migration friction
- Ownership / single-point-of-contact
- v1.5 sunset policy undecided
